# Content Strategy

## Purpose

Content strategy translates keyword research into content that satisfies user intent and supports business objectives.

This section documents the content planning process used for the CleverClimb SEO website.

## Objectives

- Create content aligned with search intent
- Build topical authority
- Improve organic visibility
- Support conversion goals
- Strengthen internal linking opportunities

## Content Types

- Service Pages
- Landing Pages
- Blog Articles
- Educational Content
- Local SEO Content
- Supporting Content

## Strategy Components

- Topic Clusters
- Content Mapping
- Search Intent Analysis
- Internal Linking
- Content Optimization

## Status

The content strategy will continue to evolve as new keyword opportunities and user needs are identified.
