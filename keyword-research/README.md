# Keyword Research

## Purpose

Keyword research forms the foundation of the CleverClimb SEO strategy.

This section documents the process used to identify, evaluate, and prioritize keywords for targeting through website content and landing pages.

## Objectives

- Identify relevant SEO keywords
- Understand search intent
- Discover content opportunities
- Evaluate keyword difficulty
- Build topic clusters

## Tools Used

- Google Keyword Planner
- Google Search
- Google Search Console
- Ahrefs (where applicable)
- SEMrush (where applicable)

## Research Areas

- Primary Keywords
- Secondary Keywords
- Long-Tail Keywords
- Local SEO Keywords
- Informational Queries
- Commercial Intent Keywords

## Status

Keyword research is ongoing and will be updated as new opportunities are identified.
