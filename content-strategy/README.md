# Content Strategy

## Purpose

Content strategy transforms keyword research into valuable content that satisfies search intent and helps achieve business objectives.

This section documents the content planning approach used for the CleverClimb SEO website.

## Objectives

- Build topical authority
- Improve organic visibility
- Create content aligned with user intent
- Support lead generation goals
- Strengthen internal linking opportunities

## Content Types

- Service Pages
- Landing Pages
- Blog Posts
- Educational Content
- Local SEO Pages
- Case Studies

## Strategy Components

- Topic Clusters
- Search Intent Analysis
- Content Mapping
- Internal Linking
- Content Optimization

## Status

The content strategy will continue to evolve as new keyword opportunities, industry trends, and user needs are identified.
