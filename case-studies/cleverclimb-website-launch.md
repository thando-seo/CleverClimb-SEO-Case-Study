# CleverClimb SEO Website Launch Case Study

## Project Overview

CleverClimb SEO is a registered SEO and digital marketing business established to provide search engine optimization services to businesses while also serving as a practical demonstration of my SEO expertise.

The website was developed as both a business asset and a real-world SEO project where I can implement, test, and refine SEO strategies. Through CleverClimb SEO, I showcase my abilities across keyword research, content strategy, on-page SEO, technical SEO, local SEO, link building, schema markup, and performance analysis.

Unlike a theoretical portfolio project, CleverClimb SEO is an active business website that allows me to apply SEO principles in a live environment and continuously improve its performance based on real search data and user behavior.

## Project Objectives

The CleverClimb SEO website was created to achieve several objectives:

- Establish a professional online presence for CleverClimb SEO as a registered business.
- Demonstrate practical SEO skills through a live website.
- Build a portfolio that showcases SEO knowledge and implementation.
- Attract potential clients seeking SEO services.
- Support career development by providing real-world SEO case studies for employers and recruiters.
- Apply and test SEO strategies in a competitive search environment.

## Business Background

CleverClimb SEO is a registered South African SEO business established to help local businesses improve their online visibility through search engine optimisation.

The website was launched in March 2026 and serves two primary purposes:

1. To attract businesses seeking professional SEO services.
2. To demonstrate practical SEO implementation through a live business website.

The project provides an opportunity to apply SEO strategies in a real-world environment while continuously testing, measuring, and refining optimisation efforts.

## Market Focus

The primary focus of CleverClimb SEO is the South African market.

A `.co.za` domain was selected to reinforce the website's local market focus and support visibility for searches relevant to South African businesses.

The target audience includes:

* Small and medium-sized businesses
* Local service providers
* Growing businesses seeking online visibility
* Organisations looking to improve their organic search performance

## Challenges

### Low Click-Through Rate (CTR)

One of the key challenges identified through performance analysis is a low click-through rate from Google Search results.

Potential contributing factors include:

- New website with limited authority
- Competitive search results
- Opportunities to improve title tags
- Opportunities to improve meta descriptions
