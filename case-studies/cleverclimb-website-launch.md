# CleverClimb SEO Website Launch Case Study

## Project Overview

CleverClimb SEO is a personal SEO and digital marketing project created to demonstrate my SEO expertise and build a professional online presence.

The website serves as both a portfolio and a practical SEO project where I can apply, test, and refine SEO strategies in a real-world environment.

## Project Objectives

- Build a professional SEO website
- Demonstrate SEO knowledge and implementation skills
- Create a portfolio for potential employers and clients
- Improve visibility for SEO-related keywords
- Gain practical experience through ongoing optimization

## Target Audience

- Small business owners
- Local businesses
- Companies seeking SEO services
- Employers hiring SEO professionals

## SEO Areas Implemented

- Keyword Research
- Content Strategy
- On-Page SEO
- Technical SEO
- Local SEO
- Link Building
- Schema Markup

## Challenges

### Low Click-Through Rate (CTR)

One of the key challenges identified through performance analysis is a low click-through rate from Google Search results.

Potential contributing factors include:

- New website with limited authority
- Competitive search results
- Opportunities to improve title tags
- Opportunities to improve meta descriptions

## Current Focus

- Improving CTR
- Expanding content coverage
- Building topical authority
- Strengthening local SEO signals
- Acquiring relevant backlinks

## Future Plans

- Publish additional SEO-focused content
- Expand location landing pages

- Continue technical SEO enhancements
